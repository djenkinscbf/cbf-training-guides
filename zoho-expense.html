Overview

What is Zoho Expense?

Zoho Expense is CBF’s expense and travel management tool, part of Zoho One. It replaces manual spreadsheets for recording spend, submitting reports, and getting reimbursed.

How CBF uses it

Staff use Zoho Expense to record business expenses, attach receipts, submit them in reports for approval, and track reimbursement. It also handles trip requests and advances, with policy checks applied automatically.

Getting Started

Accessing Zoho Expense

Open Zoho Expense in a browser. The dashboard gives you an overview of expenses, reports, trips, and pending approvals.

1
Sign in to Zoho Expense
  1. 1
    Go to expense.zoho.com
  2. 2
    Click Sign In and authenticate with your CBF account
  3. 3
    The dashboard shows your expenses, reports, trips, and approvals
Signing in at CBF: CBF accesses Zoho through Zoho One using single sign-on. Use your CBF account and complete two-factor authentication if prompted. Do not use a personal Zoho account for CBF work.
Record

Recording expenses

Add expenses manually, or let auto-scan read a receipt and fill in the details for you.

2
Add an expense
  1. 1
    Click Add Expense
  2. 2
    Choose a category (for example, meals, fuel, travel)
  3. 3
    Upload the receipt and enter the amount, date, and notes
  4. 4
    Assign it to an existing report or create a new one
Auto-scan: Upload a receipt image or PDF and Auto-scan reads the merchant, amount, and date, then fills the fields for you to confirm.
Reports

Creating and submitting reports

Group related expenses into a report and submit it for approval, then follow it through to reimbursement.

3
Submit an expense report
  1. 1
    Go to Reports and click Create Report
  2. 2
    Add the relevant expenses and any trip information
  3. 3
    Submit to your assigned approver
  4. 4
    Track the status: Submitted, Approved, Reimbursed
Travel

Trip requests and advances

Plan business travel and request money up front when needed.

4
Request a trip or advance
  1. 1
    Open Trips to request travel approval in advance
  2. 2
    Include the itinerary, estimated costs, and travel class
  3. 3
    Request an advance for expected expenses where appropriate
  4. 4
    Submit for manager and finance review
Useful Features

Features worth knowing

Recent Zoho Expense capabilities, including ones that suit a distributed team.

Real-time card feeds

Connected corporate cards can pull transactions the moment a payment is made, so reconciliation is faster and more accurate.

Auto-scan receipts

Snap or upload a receipt and let OCR capture the merchant, amount, and date automatically.

Multi-currency and per diem

Record spend in different currencies with per-diem support, useful for a team and partners spread across regions.

Policy checks

Expenses that break a configured policy are flagged at submission, so issues are caught early.

Trip Expense Summary

An analytics report that summarises the spend tied to a trip in one view.

Mobile capture

Record expenses and scan receipts on the go from the Zoho Expense mobile app.
