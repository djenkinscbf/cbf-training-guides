Overview

What is Zoho Forms?

Zoho Forms is CBF’s cloud-based form builder, part of Zoho One. It lets you create online forms to collect information, then route, store, and analyse the responses without spreadsheets or manual chasing.

How CBF uses it

At CBF, Zoho Forms is used for surveys, feedback collection, event and training registrations, and internal approval requests. You build a form once, share a link, and responses flow into one place where they can be filtered, exported, and acted on.

Getting Started

Accessing Zoho Forms

Open Zoho Forms in any browser. The dashboard shows the forms you own or collaborate on, along with ready-made templates.

1
Sign in to Zoho Forms
  1. 1
    Go to forms.zoho.com
  2. 2
    Click Sign In and authenticate with your CBF account
  3. 3
    The dashboard opens, showing your existing forms and template gallery
Signing in at CBF: CBF accesses Zoho through Zoho One using single sign-on. Use your CBF account and complete two-factor authentication if prompted. Do not use a personal Zoho account for CBF work.
Build

Creating a new form

Start from a blank form, a template, or by importing an existing form, then build it visually by dragging fields onto the canvas.

2
Create and structure a form
  1. 1
    From the dashboard, click Create Form
  2. 2
    Choose Blank Form, a Template, or Import
  3. 3
    Enter a clear name and description
  4. 4
    Drag fields from the left panel onto the canvas in the order you want them
Field tips: Use Single Line, Dropdown, Email, and Phone for everyday data. Use File Upload, Image, or Signature for richer inputs, and Formula fields to calculate values automatically.
Logic

Adding fields and rules

Fields can be configured individually, and rules let the form respond to what people enter so respondents only see what is relevant to them.

3
Configure fields and logic
  1. 1
    Click any field to set its label, placeholder, default value, and validation
  2. 2
    Use Field Rules to show or hide fields based on earlier answers
  3. 3
    Use Form Rules to trigger emails, tasks, or redirects after submission
  4. 4
    Use Page Rules on multi-page forms to branch based on responses
Share

Sharing, embedding, and collaboration

Publish a form for respondents, control who can access it, and bring colleagues in to build or manage it with you.

4
Share your form
  1. 1
    Click Share and choose a Public URL (customisable), QR Code, or tracked Email Invitation
  2. 2
    Under Embed, get an iframe, JavaScript, or pop-up snippet for a web page
  3. 3
    Set access to Public, Login-based (Zoho users only), or Domain-restricted
Collaborate: Click Collaborate in the top-right of the builder, add colleagues by email, and assign View, Edit, or Admin access so they can help manage the form and its responses.
Responses

Managing responses and reports

Every submission lands in one place where it can be reviewed, filtered, exported, and turned into visual reports.

5
Work with submitted data
  1. 1
    Open the All Entries tab to view submissions
  2. 2
    Filter and sort by field values or date
  3. 3
    Export to CSV, PDF, or Zoho Sheet
  4. 4
    Open Reports for visual summaries, then save custom report views
Useful Features

Features worth knowing

Recent additions to Zoho Forms that are useful for CBF’s surveys, approvals, and feedback workflows.

In-form signatures

A built-in Zoho Sign field lets respondents add a legally binding signature inside the form, useful for approvals and acknowledgements.

Smart Scan (OCR)

The Smart Scan field extracts and auto-fills data from an uploaded image, so people can capture details from a document instead of typing them.

Barcode and QR input

Scanner Input reads barcodes and QR codes directly in the browser and fills the field automatically.

Spotlight Forms

A focused, one-field-at-a-time layout that guides respondents smoothly through longer forms.

Auto-prefill for staff

Signed-in CBF users can have their name and email filled in automatically, saving time on internal forms.

Approval workflows

Build multi-level approvals for things like leave or expense sign-offs, with conditions and real-time status tracking, no separate tool needed.
