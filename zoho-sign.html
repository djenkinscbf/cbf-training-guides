Overview

What is Zoho Sign?

Zoho Sign is CBF’s e-signature tool, part of Zoho One. It lets you send, sign, and manage legally binding documents online, with a full audit trail.

How CBF uses it

Zoho Sign is used for onboarding paperwork, contracts, approvals, and vendor agreements. You upload a document, place signature fields, send it to recipients, and track it to completion. Recipients do not need a Zoho account to sign.

Platform
Zoho One (Sign)
Access
sign.zoho.com · browser and mobile app
Output
Legally binding signed documents and completion certificates
Signers
No Zoho account required to sign
Getting Started

Accessing Zoho Sign

Open Zoho Sign in a browser to reach your dashboard of documents and templates.

1
Sign in to Zoho Sign
  1. 1
    Go to sign.zoho.com
  2. 2
    Click Sign In and authenticate with your CBF account
  3. 3
    You are taken to the dashboard
Signing in at CBF: CBF accesses Zoho through Zoho One using single sign-on. Use your CBF account and complete two-factor authentication if prompted. Do not use a personal Zoho account for CBF work.
Setup

Setting up your profile and signature

Set your signature once so it is ready to apply to any document you sign.

2
Create your signature
  1. 1
    Click your profile icon → Settings → My Profile
  2. 2
    Upload a scanned image of your signature, draw it, or type it in a predefined font
  3. 3
    Save it so it is available on all outgoing documents
Send

Uploading and sending documents

Send a document for signature by adding recipients and placing the fields each person needs to complete.

3
Send a document for signature
  1. 1
    From the dashboard, click Send for Signature
  2. 2
    Upload a supported document (PDF, DOCX, and more)
  3. 3
    Add recipients by name and email
  4. 4
    Drag and drop signature, date, text, and checkbox fields onto the document
  5. 5
    Set the signing order if sequential, and add OTP or access-code authentication if needed
  6. 6
    Click Send — recipients are notified by email
Reuse

Templates and SignForms

For documents you send repeatedly, save a template so you do not rebuild it each time.

4
Create a reusable template
  1. 1
    Go to Templates → Create Template
  2. 2
    Upload the document and define the roles, fields, and email message
  3. 3
    Save it for reuse in recurring workflows
SignForms: A SignForm is a public, self-service link that lets people sign a standard document on demand, without you sending it one by one.
Track

Managing and tracking documents

Follow every document through its lifecycle and keep a record once it is complete.

5
Track and store documents
  1. 1
    Open the Documents tab
  2. 2
    Check statuses: Draft, Sent, In Progress, Completed, or Declined
  3. 3
    Filter by name, date, or status to find a document
  4. 4
    Open any document to view its timeline and audit trail, and download the signed copy and certificate of completion
Useful Features

Features worth knowing

Recent Zoho Sign capabilities, including ones that help a remote team get documents signed across time zones.

Sign Yourself

Sign a document yourself directly from the dashboard, without sending it to anyone.

WhatsApp sharing

Send documents for signature and authenticate signers over WhatsApp, handy when email is slow to reach someone.

Delegate signing

A recipient who is unavailable can delegate signing to someone else, keeping the process moving.

Document commenting

Add comments on a document so signers and senders can clarify points in context.

Automated reminders

Zoho Sign sends reminders to recipients who have not yet signed, so nothing stalls.

Broad integrations

Works with Zoho CRM, People, and WorkDrive, plus Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, and Dropbox.
