Overview

What is Zoho People?

Zoho People is CBF’s HR system, part of Zoho One. It holds employee records, attendance, and time off. For most staff, the day-to-day use is simple: requesting and tracking leave.

How staff use it

At CBF, staff primarily use Zoho People to request and track leave — most commonly Medical Leave and Vacation Leave. You submit a request, it routes to your approver, and you are notified once it is approved or rejected. HR and Admin manage the wider employee records behind the scenes.

Getting Started

Accessing Zoho People

Sign in from any browser, or use the mobile app for quick access while you are away from your desk. What you see depends on your role.

1
Sign in to Zoho People
  1. 1
    Go to people.zoho.com
  2. 2
    Click Sign In and authenticate with your CBF account
  3. 3
    You land on your dashboard, with the modules available to your role
Signing in at CBF: CBF accesses Zoho through Zoho One using single sign-on. Use your CBF account and complete two-factor authentication if prompted. Do not use a personal Zoho account for CBF work.
Self-Service

The employee self-service portal

The self-service portal is your personal view of Zoho People. From here you can handle most of what you need without going through HR.

Apply for and track leave

Submit Medical or Vacation Leave requests and follow their approval status.

View your profile

Check your personal details, reporting line, and stored documents.

Mark and view attendance

Check in or out and see your attendance, including your real-time status.

Use it on mobile

The same self-service is available in the Zoho People mobile app for access on the go.

Core Task

How to apply for leave

This is the task most staff use Zoho People for. The steps below cover a Medical or Vacation Leave request from start to finish.

2
Submit a leave request
  1. 1
    Sign in to Zoho People
  2. 2
    On the dashboard, open the Leave module from the left-hand menu
  3. 3
    Review the calendar view showing your leave balance and upcoming holidays
  4. 4
    Click Apply Leave (or Request Leave), usually at the top right
  5. 5
    Select the leave type — for example, Medical Leave or Vacation Leave
  6. 6
    Choose the date or dates, and set a half-day or specific hours if needed
  7. 7
    Optionally add a reason and attach supporting documents (for example, a medical certificate)
  8. 8
    Click Submit — the request routes to your manager or designated approver
Check your balance first: The Leave module shows your available balance for each leave type before you apply, so you can confirm you have enough days for the dates you need.
Track

Tracking and managing your requests

Once submitted, you can follow a request and adjust it if your plans change.

3
Follow and adjust a request
  1. 1
    You receive an email and in-app notification when the request is approved or rejected
  2. 2
    Open the Leave module at any time to see the status of pending requests
  3. 3
    If plans change, use partial cancellation to cancel only selected dates, or cancel the whole request
Useful Features

Features worth knowing

Recent Zoho People capabilities that matter for CBF, including ones suited to a fully remote team.

Remote check-in status

Attendance status now distinguishes Remote-In from Office-In, so a distributed team is reflected accurately on each profile.

Facial recognition in the browser

Facial recognition check-in is now available through the web browser, not only the mobile app.

Partial leave cancellation

Cancel just the dates you no longer need from an approved request instead of the whole thing.

Ask Your Manager

Submit HR help-desk queries straight to your reporting manager from the self-service portal.

Mobile self-service

Apply for leave, check balances, and track approvals from the Zoho People app on your phone.

Connected to Sign and Expense

Zoho People links with Zoho Sign for HR documents and Zoho Expense for reimbursements and travel approvals.
